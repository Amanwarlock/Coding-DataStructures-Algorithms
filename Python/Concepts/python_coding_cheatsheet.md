    > Negative Infinity
            - float('-inf') --> o/p -inf

    > Abs --> abs(-a)

    > Power --> pow(base, exponent)

    > Round
        - round(5.46) --> o/p 5

## Strings

    > upper-case
        - str.upper()
        - 'aman'.upper()
    
    > Lower-case
        - str.lower()
        - 'AMAN'.lower()

    > Clone a string
        - new_copy = str[:]

    > Reverse a string
        - rev_str = str[::-1]

    > .strip() - Remove whitespaces 
        - remove from front and back
            - ' I am alone '.strip() --> o/p 'I am alone'

    > .split()
        - 'hello world'.split() --> o/p ['hello', 'world']

    > str.isnumeric()
